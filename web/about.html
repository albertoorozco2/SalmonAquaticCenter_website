In the heart of Galway city. With a state-of-the-art 50m pool, designed with both the swimmer and learner in mind, heated to 28 degrees with the innovative ultra-violet pool system, reducing the amount of chlorine needed in the water.

Our staff are qualified instructors who will be available to discuss members training programmes or for any assistance. Our pool environment is also constantly manned by one of our trained lifeguards to ensure a fully safe pool environment.

It is established to be: A Venue for Competitive Swimming, Diving and Water Polo in Ireland, a Facility for the training of high performance swimmers, a host for competitions, and developing Swimming Participation at all levels.
