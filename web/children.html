CHILDREN LESSONS


We provide a comprehensive swimming instruction and lifesaving skills programme to swimmers from age 4 upwards. All of our instructors are fully qualified and experienced and we implement the Swim Ireland ‘Child Learn to Swim’ programme. This programme sets national standards for the teaching of swimming and comprises 8 levels, all of which have been specifically developed to encourage swimmers to acquire the skills necessary for swimming. It follows a progressive syllabus with each child earning certificates as they move upwards through the levels.
