CHANGING ROOMS


The changing rooms cover a total area of 850 square metres and are divided into four differentiated zones.
Changing Area - Includes the place where the swimmers get kitted up, with each of their lockers, while the rest of the area contains baths and showers and a storeroom for sports materials.
Water Area - Includes a jacuzzis, a sauna, a steam bath, a cold bath and a relaxation area.
Treatment Area - It is used by the doctors, and physiotherapists.
Technical Area - Contains three offices, a meeting room and the coaching staff's changing rooms.
