ADULTS LESSONS


Our Adult Swimming Lessons are a great introduction to swimming, whether you are looking to take the plunge as a complete beginner, or if you are returning to swimming and want to improve your stroke and technique. We offer the following classes
Nervous Adults - for those who are fearful of water. The class will move at a slow pace, with teacher: pupil ratios kept very low.
Adult Beginner - for those who have no swimming skills.
Adult Improvers - for those who can move through the water but have no defined stroke pattern.
Adult Profesionals - for those who want training for competitions or improve swimming skills.
