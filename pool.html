SWIMMING POOL


Galway's newest and state-of-the-art 50m pool opened in July 2013. The revolutionary design and mechanics allow the pool depth to change to accommodate swimmers from all levels of ability, and heated to 28 degrees. The innovative ultra-violet pool system treatment and filtration technology means the chlorine levels are so low you will feel like you are swimming in fresh spring water. Even better still, you will never smell of chlorine after your swim. Offering you the very best swimming experience.
